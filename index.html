Step 03 · The Letter

Send it in your own words

Edit freely — a letter in your own words carries more weight than a form letter. Personal stakes ("as a parent…", "as someone who works in…") matter most.

Why this works

MPs and senators weigh correspondence from the people they represent — it is tracked, tallied, and answered. A cited, specific letter cannot be dismissed as noise.

Every claim in this letter carries the section number of the bill it comes from, so the office can verify it in minutes.

The letter opens in your mail app and is sent from your address.

Recorded: nothing

No accounts, no analytics, no cookies, no database. The letter is sent by you, from your own email — this page never sees it, or you.
