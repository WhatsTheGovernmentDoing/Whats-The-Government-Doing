Get the app.

This site installs straight from your browser — free, no app store, and it updates itself. It records nothing about you, installed or not.

Take Action The Record Get the App
Two taps · Pick your device

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button ⬆︎ (bottom of the screen).
  3. Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen, then Add.

The maple-stamp icon lands on your home screen and opens full-screen, like any app.

Android

  1. Open this site in Chrome.
  2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right).
  3. Tap Add to Home screen → Install.

Chrome may also offer an Install app banner directly — that works too.

Computer

  1. Open this site in Chrome or Edge.
  2. Click the install icon at the right end of the address bar (a screen with a ↓ arrow).
  3. Click Install.

It opens in its own window and sits in your taskbar / dock like any program.

Why no app store? Because this way is better: nothing to pay, nothing to review, updates arrive the moment we publish them — and the promise on every page holds here too: recorded: nothing.
